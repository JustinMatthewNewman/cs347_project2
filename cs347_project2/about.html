📜 Manifesto

We are mission driven and process focused group of young web developers looking to hone our skills and practices within the design, collaboration, and implementation of web pages. We strive to put our technical and interpersonal skills to use within a group of JMU students to help create awesome web pages for whomever might be in need of one!


As a team member you will be working with and subject to the caring and passionate individuals who create fun environments surrounding real world skill in Data Analysis, Calculus, Discrete Structures, Java, Python, OpenCV, DataScience, Statistics, and iOS mobile application development.


🌱 We love learning C, Assembly, SQL, Database Management, and SwiftUI for the Apple development program.


💞️ We are looking to collaborate on Data Science projects for openCV in python3 and iOS mobile application development with Swift code and SwiftUI.
